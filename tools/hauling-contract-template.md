## 4. Payment Terms

### 4.1 Invoicing

Carrier will submit invoices [weekly / bi-weekly / monthly] with copies of all supporting BOLs and scale tickets.

### 4.2 Payment Due

Payment is due **Net 30 days** from invoice date. Invoices not disputed within 10 business days of receipt are deemed accepted.

### 4.3 Late Payment

Unpaid invoices past 30 days will accrue interest at the rate of **1.5% per month** (18% per annum) on the outstanding balance. Carrier reserves the right to suspend service if any invoice remains unpaid beyond 45 days.

### 4.4 Disputed Invoices

Customer must notify Carrier in writing of any disputed charges within 10 business days of invoice receipt. Undisputed portions remain due per the original payment schedule. The parties will work in good faith to resolve disputes within 15 business days.

## 6. Responsibilities and Compliance

### 6.1 Carrier Responsibilities

- Provide properly licensed CDL drivers (Class A or B as applicable)
- Maintain all vehicles in safe, DOT-compliant condition
- Maintain valid USDOT number, MC Authority (if interstate), and Mississippi Harvest Permit
- Comply with all FMCSA regulations including driver qualification files, hours of service, and drug & alcohol testing
- Secure and tarp all loads as required by law and site rules
- Provide on-time delivery per dispatch schedule
- Maintain clean CSA safety scores

### 6.2 Customer Responsibilities

- Provide safe, accessible loading and unloading areas
- Ensure material is available for loading at scheduled times
- Provide accurate material descriptions including any hazardous material disclosures
- Designate an on-site contact for delivery coordination
- Not direct or instruct Carrier's drivers in a manner that would violate DOT regulations

## 7. Indemnification and Liability

### 7.1 Carrier Indemnification

Carrier shall indemnify and hold harmless Customer from claims, damages, or liabilities arising from Carrier's negligence or willful misconduct in performing services under this Agreement, including but not limited to vehicle accidents, property damage, and environmental contamination caused by Carrier's equipment.

### 7.2 Customer Indemnification

Customer shall indemnify and hold harmless Carrier from claims, damages, or liabilities arising from Customer's negligence, including but not limited to unsafe site conditions, inaccurate material descriptions, and undisclosed hazardous materials.

### 7.3 Limitation of Liability

Neither party shall be liable for consequential, incidental, or punitive damages. Carrier's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Customer in the 12 months preceding the claim.
