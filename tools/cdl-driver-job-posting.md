### Job Description

[Company Name] is a Mississippi aggregate hauling company serving the largest construction boom in state history — over $49 billion in data center projects across the state. We are hiring experienced Class A CDL drivers to haul crushed stone, gravel, sand, road base, and fill dirt from quarries to major construction sites.

This is steady, year-round work. Data center construction runs on aggressive timelines with consistent daily loads — no sitting around waiting for dispatch.

### What You'll Do

- Haul aggregate materials from quarries to construction sites (short and medium haul, most loads under 50 miles)
- Operate day cab tractor with belly dump, end dump, or side dump trailers
- Operate tandem axle dump trucks as needed
- Complete 6-10 loads per day depending on route distance
- Perform pre-trip and post-trip vehicle inspections daily
- Secure and tarp loads per DOT requirements
- Maintain accurate Bills of Lading and delivery documentation

### Schedule

- Monday through Friday, typical hours 5:30 AM - 5:00 PM
- Quarry gates open at 6:00 AM — early starts are the norm
- Home every night — no OTR, no overnight runs
- Saturday work available when you want extra hours

### Equipment

You'll operate well-maintained, late-model equipment:

- **Tractors:** Kenworth T880, Peterbilt 567, or equivalent day cabs
- **Trailers:** Belly dump, end dump, and side dump trailers
- **Dump trucks:** Kenworth T880, Peterbilt 567, or Mack Granite tandem axle

We maintain our equipment on schedule — you won't be driving junk.

### Requirements

- **Valid Class A CDL** with air brake endorsement
- Clean driving record (no DUI/DWI in past 5 years, no more than 2 moving violations in past 3 years)
- Minimum 1 year of dump truck or aggregate hauling experience preferred (will consider CDL school graduates with strong references)
- Must be 21+ years old (interstate hauling)
- Must pass DOT pre-employment drug screen and physical
- Ability to tarp and secure loads
- Reliable attendance — show up on time, every day

### Nice to Have

- Experience with belly dump or end dump trailers
- Familiarity with Mississippi quarries (APAC, Rogers Group, Vulcan, Lafarge)
- Mississippi Harvest Permit experience (84,000 lbs)

### What We Offer

- **Pay above market:** $22-$26/hr (MS average for dump truck drivers is $21.81/hr)
- **Consistent work:** Data center projects provide steady loads year-round
- **Home every night:** Local hauling only, no OTR
- **Well-maintained equipment:** Late-model trucks kept on a preventive maintenance schedule
- **Steady hours:** 45-55 hours/week available
- **Growth opportunity:** We're scaling our fleet — experienced drivers can move into lead driver or dispatcher roles
- **Drug & alcohol testing program** per FMCSA requirements

### How to Apply

Apply online or call [Phone Number] to schedule an interview.

Bring to interview:
- Valid Class A CDL
- Current DOT medical card
- Driving record (MVR) from the past 3 years
