# Launch Checklist — Mississippi Aggregate Hauling Company

> Track your progress: [ ] = not started | [~] = in progress | [x] = done

---

## PHASE 1: FORMATION (Week 1-2)

### Business Entity
- [ ] Choose company name (check availability at sos.ms.gov)
- [ ] File Certificate of Formation with MS Secretary of State ($50, online only)
- [ ] Receive Certificate of Formation (1-2 business days)
- [ ] Obtain EIN from IRS (free, online at irs.gov, instant)
- [ ] Open business bank account (bring: EIN letter, Certificate of Formation, personal ID)
- [ ] Order business checks
- [ ] Set up accounting software (QuickBooks Self-Employed or QuickBooks Simple Start)

### Federal Registrations
- [ ] Apply for USDOT number at safer.fmcsa.dot.gov (free, instant)
- [ ] Apply for MC Authority if hauling interstate ($300)
- [ ] File BOC-3 (process agent designation) through filing service ($30-$150)
- [ ] Note: MC Authority takes 3-4 weeks to activate — start early

### State Registrations
- [ ] Register with MS Department of Revenue (dor.ms.gov)
- [ ] Register with MS Dept of Employment Security (mdes.ms.gov) — if hiring
- [ ] Apply for IFTA license and decals (requires USDOT + TIN first)
- [ ] Apply for IRP apportioned plates if operating multi-state
- [ ] Apply for UCR registration at plan.ucr.gov ($46)

## PHASE 2: INSURANCE & PERMITS (Week 2-3)

### Insurance
- [ ] Get 3+ liability insurance quotes (minimum $750K interstate / $300K intrastate)
- [ ] Get cargo insurance quotes (~$100K coverage)
- [ ] Get physical damage/collision quotes for trucks
- [ ] Select insurer and bind coverage
- [ ] File BMC-91 or BMC-91X with FMCSA (required to activate MC Authority)
- [ ] Obtain Certificate of Insurance for quarries/customers
- [ ] Research workers' comp providers (required at 5+ employees)

### Permits
- [ ] Apply for Mississippi Harvest Permit ($25/vehicle) at permits.mdot.ms.gov
  - Create account → upload insurance → get MDOT approval → purchase permit
  - Phone: 888-737-0061 or 601-359-1717
- [ ] File Form 2290 (HVUT) at irs.gov for each vehicle ($100-$550/vehicle)
- [ ] Receive stamped Schedule 1 from IRS (needed for vehicle registration)
